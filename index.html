Why use TEXTKING?

TEXTKING is built for students, bloggers, and writers who need a reliable tool for text processing. From word counting to exporting professional PDFs, we've got you covered.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TEXTKING free to use?

Yes! All features including PDF export, voice engine, and text transformations are 100% free with no registration required.

Does it store my text?

No. TEXTKING processes everything locally in your browser. We never see or store your content on our servers.

How to convert text to PDF?

Simply type or paste your text into the editor and click the "Save .pdf" button in the Export panel.
